Moti is an interactive friend stimulating & engaging children in educational activities

Moti is entirely based on sensory stimulation: it talks, plays music, rolls around, emits lights, vibrations… Using the demonstrated power of robotics, Moti can adapt to help children progress on a large range of skills.

Interaction

Because without interaction there is no progress, Moti facilitates interaction through stimulation & play

Autonomy

Moti brings time structure & motivate children to complete daily tasks by gamifying every situation and guide them to success

Therapist Tool

Moti is designed according to validated comportemental & relationships methods (ABA, TEACCH…)

Through Testing we already showed high interest, we are gonna show progress !
From day one we have spent a lot of time with children to better understand their needs and test our prototype. We are now mentored by a closed scientific advisory board
Our main goal is to demonstrate by proofs that robotic is an effective tool to reduce anxiety and enhance autism therapies. We want to provide a robotic toy that behind its fun and playing proprieties include functionnalities proven to be helpful for those in the autism spectrum. You can learn more about evidence-based practices guidelines on the website of the [National Professional Development Center on Autism Spectrum Disorder](http://autismpdc.fpg.unc.edu/evidence-based-practices).
First tests with 70 children showed they are : More interested in the robot than with classic toys, Motivated to play all along the 15 minutes session, Communicative & joyful when playing with the robot.
